Capabilities

Everything in one firmware.

From WiFi recon to RF experimentation to on-device forensics — all running on a $5 microcontroller.

📡
WiFi Recon
Passive & active wireless scanning, packet capture, deauth detection, and probe request tracking.
📻
RF24 Experimentation
NRF24L01+ integration for ISM band analysis, spectrum mapping, and packet injection testing.
🔍
BLE Scanning
Bluetooth Low Energy device enumeration, advertisement analysis, and GATT service discovery.
💾
Forensic Capture
PCAP recording to SD card, timestamped logs, Wireshark-compatible export, and hash-verified integrity.
📊
Telemetry Output
MQTT and HTTP telemetry for real-time dashboards, plus serial output for local monitoring.
🔧
Audit Tools
Network enumeration, service fingerprinting, GPIO-triggered events, and hardware sensor integration.
